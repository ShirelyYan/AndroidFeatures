SUMMARY
Trojan:Android/Mseg silently sends SMS messages to premium-rate numbers. It also collects data from the device and forwards it to a remote server.
TECHNICAL DETAILS
Once installed, Trojan:Android/Mseg will collect the following information from the device:
• International Mobile Equipment Identity (IMEI) number
• Telephone number
• Contacts details
These details are silently forwarded to a remote server.
In addition, the app silently sends SMS messages to premium-rate numbers.
