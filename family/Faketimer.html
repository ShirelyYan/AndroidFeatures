SUMMARY
Trojan:Android/FakeTimer installs a service on the device that provides access to an adult website; it also forwards details of the device to a remote server.
TECHNICAL DETAILS
Trojan:Android/FakeTimer installs a service named .KitchenTimerService. onto the device, and accesses a pornography website. It also retrieves the device's phone number and device ID, which it later forwards to a remote server.
