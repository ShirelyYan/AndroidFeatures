SUMMARY
MobileTX.A retrieves data from a compromised device, and attempts to connect to a remote site.
TECHNICAL DETAILS
MobileTX.A retrieves the compromised device's International Mobile Subscriber Identity (IMSI) number, and then attempts to connect to a remote site,http://[...]mobile.tx.com.cn[...].
Based on the response from the site, the application may attempt to send an SMS message containing the IMSI number to a specified number.
MobileTX.A managed to forward the device's IMSI number before it crashed.
