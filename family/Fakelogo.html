SUMMARY
FakeLogo variants silently sends SMS messages containing text content to specific phone numbers.
TECHNICAL DETAILS
FakeLogo variants send SMS messages with specifically formulated text content to certain numbers. At the time of writing, there are two known variants: FakeLogo.A and FakeLogo.B.
FakeLogo.A sends SMS messages with text content in the following format:
• .h 1107[1/2/3/4][random].4
to the number, 1899.
FakeLogo.B sends SMS messages with the following text content:
• .75117132 + 4225.
to multiple phone numbers. In addition, FakeLogo.B will verify the SMS delivery using the standard SMS sending verification function.
