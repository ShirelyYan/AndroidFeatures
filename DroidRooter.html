• SUMMARY
    DroidRooter is a family of binary exploits that is used to gain root privilege on an Android device.
    TECHNICAL DETAILS
    Exploits from the Droidrooter family are binary exploits that could be used by an Android malware to gain root privilege on a compromised device. Even though it is classified as an exploit, under certain circumstances, these exploits may be considered as a hack-tool when users deliberately run them on their own phone in order to 'jailbreak' or unlock the device.
